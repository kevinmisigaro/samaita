About Us

Samaita was established in 2014 by Israel Chasosa, a seasoned career banker, as an advisory boutique which commenced operations in 2019.

Samaita’s mission is to provide holistic advisory services to enterprises and impact investment funds. Samaita has a special focus on directing optimal capital to investment opportunities that contribute to improving lives, job opportunities and overall economic development.

Samaita mobilizes investments (capital and debt) from within and outside Tanzania and works with a portfolio of investors and financiers. Samaita acts as the eyes, ears and knowledge on the ground for investors / financiers Samaita can support pre and post-placement activities from source transactions, carry out due diligence, present proposals for consideration and, lastly, monitor the projects throughout the lifespan of the investment.

Samaita’s principle is focused on developing and maintaining long term client relationships built on trust, and a commitment to delivering the highest quality outcomes for our clients.
